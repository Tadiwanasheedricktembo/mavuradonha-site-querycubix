Life at Mavuradonha High School

Mavuradonha High School is a mission school located in Mt Darwin, Mashonaland Central Province, Zimbabwe. Run by the Evangelical Church of Zimbabwe, we are committed to providing quality education in a Christian environment. Our institution offers boarding facilities for both boys and girls, fostering a disciplined and structured learning atmosphere.
