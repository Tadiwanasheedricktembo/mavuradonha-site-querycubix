Admissions

Start your journey with Mavuradonha High School. Learn about our admission process and requirements.

How to Apply

1

Research Programs

Explore our academic programs from Form 1 to Form 6 and choose the stream that best fits your academic goals and interests.

2

Check Requirements

Review the admission requirements for your chosen level and ensure you meet all criteria including age and academic prerequisites.

3

Prepare Documents

Gather all required documents including birth certificate, previous school reports, and any other required documentation.

4

Submit Application

For Form One, apply through the EMAP platform. For Lower Six, visit the school with your O-Level results and required documents.

5

Acceptance

Receive your admission decision and complete enrollment procedures including payment of initial fees if accepted.

Admission Requirements

Form One Admission

  • Completed Grade 7 with good academic standing
  • Age appropriate for Form One (typically 13-14 years)
  • Birth certificate and previous school reports
  • Application through EMAP platform
  • Initial payment of $1,200 (includes uniforms, books, etc.)
  • Parent/guardian consent and involvement

Lower Six Admission

  • Completed O-Level with minimum required subjects
  • O-Level results slip showing subject grades
  • Age appropriate for Advanced Level
  • On-site application with required documents
  • Term fees of $500 per term
  • Academic interview may be required

Boarding Requirements

  • Medical examination and health records
  • Personal items and bedding
  • Parent/guardian emergency contact information
  • Adherence to school rules and regulations
  • All meals provided by the school
