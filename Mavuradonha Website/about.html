About Mavuradonha High School

Discover our story, mission, and commitment to excellence in education

Our Mission

To provide quality secondary education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for academic success and responsible citizenship in Zimbabwe and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a leading secondary school in Zimbabwe, recognized for academic excellence, character development, and commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society.

Our History

Founded in 1994, Mavuradonha High School has been at the forefront of educational excellence in Mashonaland Central for over 30 years. What started as a small school with just 50 students has grown into one of the most respected secondary schools in the region.

Throughout our journey, we have remained committed to our core values of academic excellence, character development, and community service. Our campus has expanded significantly, and we now serve over 800 students annually from Form 1 to Form 6.

We take pride in our rich history of producing successful graduates who have gone on to excel in various fields, from medicine and engineering to business and the arts, both in Zimbabwe and internationally.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from teaching and learning to character development and community engagement.

Respect

We foster a culture of mutual respect, embracing diversity and creating an inclusive environment where all students feel valued and supported.

Innovation

We encourage creativity and innovation, preparing students to adapt to the ever-changing demands of the modern world.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of academic and personal integrity, fostering trust and respect in our school community.
